Mozzarella Sticks

Description

Mozzarella sticks are elongated pieces of battered or breaded mozzarella, usually served as hors d'oeuvre.

Ingredients

  • 400g block mozzarella (not in water)
  • 50g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g breadcrumbs
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • vegetable or sunflower oil for frying dips , to serve
