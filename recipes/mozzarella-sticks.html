Mozzarella Sticks

Description

Mozzarella sticks are elongated pieces of battered or breaded mozzarella, usually served as hors d'oeuvre.

Ingredients

  • 400g block mozzarella (not in water)
  • 50g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g breadcrumbs
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • vegetable or sunflower oil for frying dips , to serve

Instructions

  1. Cut the mozzarella into chips.
  2. Tip the flour into a bowl and season well.
  3. Whisk the eggs in a second bowl and tip the breadcrumbs, paprika and oregano into a third.
  4. Coat the mozzarella sticks in the flour, then the egg, then the breadcrumbs.
  5. Transfer to a parchment-lined tray and freeze for at least 2 hrs.
  6. Heat enough oil to come 2-3cm up the side of a saucepan.
  7. when hot, drop in a test chip it should sizzle straight away and brown within a minute or two.
  8. The secret is to cook them long enough to crisp up the outside without letting the mozzarella ooze out.
  9. Cook the chips in batches and drain on kitchen paper. Serve hot with your favourite dip.
